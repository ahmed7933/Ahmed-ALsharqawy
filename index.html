Creative Research
Data-Driven Foundation
$0 – $10K +
STEP 01
Internal Brand Audit
Mining Historical Data
  • → Analyze the best and worst-performing ads of the last 30-90 days using AI tools.
  • → Hunt for behavioral patterns, not just 'good or bad' subjective opinions.
  • → Deconstruct winning Hooks to understand the underlying psychological drivers.
  • → Map the entire funnel from ad click to landing page conversion.
  • → Mine customer pain points from reviews and support tickets via AI sentiment analysis.
💡 Takes 1 Day – Internal data is 100% accurate.
STEP 02
Audience Intelligence
Psychographic Deep Dive
  • → Identity: Who are they? How do they perceive themselves?
  • → Motivation: What is the emotional driver pushing them to buy right now?
  • → Buying Context: What solutions have they tried before and why did they fail?
  • → Determine the Customer Awareness Level (from the 5 core stages).
💡 Depth wins – We target psychological profiles, not just demographics.
STEP 03
Competitor Research
Market Gap Analysis
  • → Scrape Meta Ad Library and sort competitors by highest active impressions.
  • → Direct Competitors → Understand the current market baseline and offers.
  • → Indirect Competitors → Steal high-converting creative frameworks from other niches.
💡 Done last – External data is less reliable than your own.
📋
Strategic Briefing
Engineering the Angle
$10K – $100K +
STEP 04
Brief Architecture
7-Step Brief Workflow
  • → Extract proven winning elements from the data bank.
  • → Strictly define: ONE Audience + ONE Angle per brief.
  • → Write the script using the GBP framework (Gut → Brain → Pocket).
  • → Design 3-4 variations to isolate specific variables.
💡 A flawless brief eliminates guesswork in the editing room.
STEP 05
EPIC Angle Framework
Angle Generation Matrix
  • → E – Emotional: How do we make them feel something immediately?
  • → P – Practical: How does this technically solve their core problem?
  • → I – Identity: Who are they as a person? Does this validate their status?
  • → C – Critical: What is the severe cost of ignoring this solution?
💡 The secret is prolonging a winning angle's lifespan, not reinventing it daily.

  • → Shoot in isolated Scenes, not continuous, uneditable videos.

  • → Modular Editing: Treat clips like Lego blocks to generate massive output.

Fatal Scaling Traps

The most common strategic and operational errors that torch budgets and break algorithms.

⚠️
Shiny Object Syndrome
Constantly jumping to new products before fully cracking and saturating a single hero offer.
⚠️
The Data Fog
Launching multiple audiences, offers, and creative styles in a single campaign, making it impossible to attribute success.
⚠️
Low-Fidelity Briefs
Blaming the video editor for poor performance when the actual issue is a brief lacking a psychological angle.
